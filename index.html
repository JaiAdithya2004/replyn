How it works

Retrieve, generate, measure.

Three steps, no model training. Retrieval keeps replies on-brand; measurement keeps them honest.

STEP 01 · RETRIEVE

Ground in past replies

For each email, Replyn finds the most similar past support cases and feeds them to the model as examples — so it answers in your team's voice and facts. This is RAG, not fine-tuning.

STEP 02 · GENERATE

Draft the reply

An LLM writes one concise, professional reply. A three-provider fallback chain (Groq → OpenRouter → Gemini) means a rate-limited provider never blocks a draft.

STEP 03 · MEASURE

Score how good it is

Every reply is graded on semantic similarity to the gold answer plus an LLM-judge rubric — helpfulness, correctness, tone, completeness — for a per-reply and overall score.

Measuring quality — the hard part

A support reply has no single right answer. So exact-match is the wrong ruler.

Many replies can all be excellent. Overlap metrics like BLEU punish good paraphrases and reward copying. Replyn scores what actually matters — and validates that the score tracks real quality.

◎ Two complementary axes

Semantic similarity (30%) — did the draft cover the same substance as the human reply, rewarding meaning over exact words. LLM-judge rubric (70%) — helpfulness, correctness, tone, completeness, graded against the gold reply at temperature 0.

✓ We validate the ruler itself

A metric is only trustworthy if it separates good from bad. Replyn scores known gold / paraphrase / generic / wrong replies and checks the order holds — 98% ranking accuracy across 39 emails. Evidence, not a vibe.

After downloading

Load it in Chrome

  1. Unzip the downloaded file.
  2. Open chrome://extensions, turn on Developer mode.
  3. Click Load unpacked, then select the unzipped replyn-extension folder.
  4. Open the popup, set your Backend URL, pick a tone, Save.
  5. In Gmail, hit Reply, then click the floating AI button.
